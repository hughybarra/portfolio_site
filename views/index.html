A Little Bit About Me

Hey how goes it? My name is Hugh Ybarra and your visiting my portfolio website. I’m originally from Tucson Arizona but spent most of my childhood growing up in Mexico City. I enjoy travelling and exploring the world. I'm always looking for some change, something new and exciting, and something to keep me busy.

You could say that I’m a bit of a foodie, and coffee shop junkie. You can usually find me spending my free time in a busy coffee shop pounding espresso and hacking away at my computer. I enjoy coding a lot, Its my new found passion. I used to play video games but found that I enjoy making them more than I like playing them.

I recently graduated from Full Sail University with a degree in Web Design and Development, and im trying to get my start in the web development industry. I’m glad to be finished and ready to start working with what I have learned

I’m a veteran of the United States Army. I used to be a 19Kilo (M1 Armor Crewman) which means I used to drive tanks. Yup that right I said tanks. I was active duty for four years and national guard for two. While in, I got to do all of the fun soldiering stuff , and all of the other not so much fun that goes along with soldiering. I deployed to Iraq for 15 months and got to travel all over that country. I was stationed in Fort Hood Texas, Natick Labs, Fort Knox, FOB Marez/Diamondback, and Fob Sykes. I really enjoyed being a soldier. The army made me the man I am today.

That just about sums up the last ten years in a nutshell. if you have any questions or would like to know something more about me feel free to get in touch I would love to hear from you.
